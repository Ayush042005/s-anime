Rascal Does not Dream of a Bunny Girl

Rascal Does Not Dream of Bunny Girl Senpai follows high school student Sakuta Azusagawa as he encounters Mai Sakurajima, a famous actress who has inexplicably become invisible to others. This strange phenomenon, dubbed "Adolescence Syndrome," is linked to the emotional struggles and social pressures faced by teenagers. Sakuta, having experienced the syndrome himself, vows to help Mai and other girls who are afflicted by these supernatural manifestations of their psychological issues. The series uses its unique blend of slice-of-life and supernatural elements to explore themes of social anxiety, self-acceptance, and the power of human connection, all while featuring the developing romance between Sakuta and Mai.

Vinland Saga

"Vinland Saga" is a historical manga and anime series following the life of Thorfinn, a young Viking warrior consumed by a quest for revenge against Askeladd, the man responsible for his father's death. The first season, the "War Arc", depicts Thorfinn's journey with Askeladd's mercenary band as they participate in conflicts and political intrigue, showcasing the brutality of the Viking age. The second season, the "Slave Arc", takes a shift in tone as Thorfinn becomes a slave after Askeladd's death and embarks on a path towards finding peace and meaning in life, influenced by his father's words and a new friend, Einar. The story explores themes of vengeance, honor, violence, pacifism, and the search for a true warrior's path.
