Rascal Does not Dream of a Bunny Girl

Rascal Does Not Dream of Bunny Girl Senpai follows Sakuta as he helps Mai, a famous actress invisible to others due to a mysterious phenomenon called "Adolescence Syndrome." The series explores teen anxieties, emotional trauma, and self-acceptance through a supernatural slice-of-life narrative.

Vinland Saga

"Vinland Saga" follows Thorfinn, a Viking youth bent on revenge. Through war and slavery, he learns to redefine his path from hatred to peace. The anime explores vengeance, violence, honor, and transformation, set against brutal Viking history.

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu No Yaiba

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba follows Tanjiro Kamado as he joins the Demon Slayer Corps after his family is killed and his sister, Nezuko, is turned into a demon. The series is a supernatural action-adventure that explores themes of family bonds, loss, revenge, perseverance, and the fight against evil through thrilling battles and emotional character development..
